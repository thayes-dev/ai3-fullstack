# Expense Reports — Submission, Limits, and Appeals

## Submission Process
1. Log into the Finance portal at finance.northbrookpartners.com
2. Click "New Expense Report"
3. Enter trip/purpose details
4. Upload receipts for each line item (photo or PDF)
5. Submit for manager approval

## Submission Deadline
All expense reports must be submitted within **30 calendar days** of the expense date. Reports submitted after 30 days require VP-level approval and may be denied.

## Reimbursement Limits

### Hotels
| City Category | Nightly Limit |
|---------------|--------------|
| Standard cities | $200/night |
| High-cost cities (NYC, San Francisco, Chicago, Boston, DC, LA, Seattle) | $275/night |

Rates above the limit require pre-approval from your department VP.

### Meals
| Meal | Limit |
|------|-------|
| Breakfast | $20 |
| Lunch | $35 |
| Dinner | $60 |
| Team dinner (with client) | $100/person |

### Transportation
- Economy airfare only (upgrades at employee's expense)
- Rental cars: compact or midsize only
- Rideshare: reasonable and documented
- Mileage: $0.67/mile (IRS 2025 rate)

## Common Rejection Reasons
1. **Missing receipts** — every item over $25 requires a receipt
2. **Over limit** — amount exceeds category cap without pre-approval
3. **Late submission** — past the 30-day window
4. **Insufficient description** — "misc" or "business expense" is not acceptable; include who, what, and why

## Appeals Process
If your report is rejected:
1. Check the rejection reason in the Finance portal (click on the report → "Status Details")
2. Add missing information or receipts
3. Resubmit with a note explaining the correction
4. If you disagree with the rejection, email your Finance Business Partner

Reports are typically processed within 5 business days of approval. Reimbursement appears on your next pay cycle.
