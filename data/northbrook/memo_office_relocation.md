# Northbrook Partners — Internal Memo

**To:** All Employees
**From:** David Park, COO
**Date:** February 3, 2025
**Subject:** Office Relocation — 250 Innovation Drive

---

Team,

As Sarah mentioned in her annual kickoff message, Northbrook Partners will be relocating our Chicago headquarters from 100 Main Street to **250 Innovation Drive** in March 2025. This memo provides the details you need to prepare for the transition.

## Why We Are Moving

Our current office at 100 Main Street has served us well for over six years, but we have outgrown the space. With 320 employees and plans to hire significantly in 2025, the 32,000-square-foot office no longer meets our needs. The new space at 250 Innovation Drive offers **45,000 square feet** — a 40% increase — in a modern building with amenities that support the way we work today.

## New Office Highlights

The new headquarters includes:

- **Open collaboration areas** on every floor, designed for team meetings, whiteboard sessions, and informal brainstorming.
- **60 private focus rooms** for deep work, calls, and small meetings (up from 22 at our current office).
- A **dedicated event space** on the ground floor that seats up to 150 people for all-hands meetings, client events, and training sessions.
- A **full-service cafe** operated by a local catering company, with subsidized lunch options for employees.
- **Improved parking** with 180 reserved spaces in the attached garage (currently we have 110).
- Floor-to-ceiling windows with views of Lincoln Park on the upper floors.

The office layout has been designed with our hybrid work model in mind. Every team will have a designated "home zone" with assigned desks for in-office days, plus shared hoteling desks for flexibility. Conference rooms are equipped with upgraded video conferencing technology to ensure a seamless experience for remote participants.

## What You Need to Do

1. **Pack your personal items** using the labeled boxes that will be delivered to your desk the week of February 10. Label each box with your name and new desk assignment (you will receive your desk assignment by February 7).
2. **Do not pack your computer, monitors, or peripherals.** IT will handle all technology equipment separately to ensure everything is properly reconnected at the new location.
3. **Clean out shared spaces** (kitchen cabinets, storage closets, supply rooms) during the week of February 17. Items not labeled by February 21 will be donated or discarded.
4. **Update your address** in the HR portal and on any business cards or client-facing materials by March 1.

## Parking During the Transition

During the move weekend (March 1-2), the parking garage at 250 Innovation Drive will be reserved for moving trucks. Starting March 3, employees can use the attached garage. Parking access cards will be distributed at the front desk on your first day.

For the first two weeks (March 3-14), overflow parking is available at the Lincoln Park municipal lot, a 5-minute walk from the office. Northbrook Partners will cover the parking cost during this period — simply submit your parking receipts through the expense portal.

## Public Transportation

The new office is served by the Brown and Purple Line (Diversey station, 8-minute walk) and multiple CTA bus routes. If you currently take the Red Line, the transfer adds approximately 5 minutes to your commute. A bike room with 20 racks is available on the ground floor.

## Questions and Concerns

I understand that office moves can be disruptive, and we have worked hard to make this as smooth as possible. If you have questions or concerns, please reach out to the Office Move Team at officemove@northbrookpartners.com. We will also hold drop-in Q&A sessions on February 12 and February 19 at 12:00 PM in Conference Room A.

Thank you for your patience and flexibility. I am excited about our new home and confident that it will support our growth for years to come.

David
