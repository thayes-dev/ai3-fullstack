# Northbrook Partners — Remote and Hybrid Work Policy

**Policy Number:** OPS-2024-007
**Effective Date:** June 1, 2024
**Department:** Operations
**Approved By:** David Park, COO

---

## Purpose

Northbrook Partners has adopted a hybrid work model that balances the benefits of in-person collaboration with the flexibility of remote work. This policy outlines expectations, eligibility, and support for employees participating in the hybrid work arrangement.

## Hybrid Work Schedule

All eligible employees are expected to work **3 days per week in the office** and may work **2 days per week remotely**. The standard in-office days are Tuesday, Wednesday, and Thursday. Employees may request alternative in-office schedules through their manager, subject to team needs and coverage requirements.

Managers have the discretion to require additional in-office days during peak periods, client engagements, or team events. Such requirements should be communicated at least one week in advance when possible.

Fully remote arrangements are available only in exceptional circumstances and require VP-level approval. As of the effective date of this policy, fully remote roles are limited to employees in regions where Northbrook Partners does not maintain a physical office.

## Eligibility

All full-time employees who have completed their probationary period are eligible for hybrid work. New employees are expected to work in the office full-time during their first 90 days to support onboarding and team integration. Contractors and temporary employees follow the terms of their individual agreements.

## Remote Work Equipment Stipend

To support productive remote work, Northbrook Partners provides a one-time **equipment stipend of $1,500** for each eligible employee. This stipend is intended to cover the cost of a home office setup, including items such as a desk, ergonomic chair, monitor, keyboard, mouse, and other peripherals.

The stipend is available upon completion of the probationary period and is disbursed as a reimbursement against receipts submitted through the Finance portal. Employees who voluntarily leave the company within 12 months of receiving the stipend may be required to repay a prorated portion.

An annual refresh stipend of **$300** is available for replacing worn equipment or purchasing accessories. Refresh stipend requests open each January.

## Technology and Security Requirements

### VPN

All employees working remotely **must connect to the company VPN** before accessing any internal systems, including email, file storage, project management tools, and development environments. The VPN client is pre-installed on company-issued laptops. Employees using personal devices must install the approved VPN client and follow the configuration guide provided by IT.

### Devices

Company-issued laptops are the preferred device for all work. Employees who use personal devices for remote work must ensure the device meets minimum security standards: current operating system with automatic updates enabled, approved antivirus software, disk encryption enabled, and a screen lock with a timeout of no more than 5 minutes.

### Network

Employees are responsible for maintaining a reliable internet connection for remote work. Northbrook Partners does not reimburse internet service costs but provides the equipment stipend to offset home office expenses.

## Communication and Availability

Remote employees must be available during core business hours (10:00 AM to 3:00 PM local time) via Slack and email. Employees should keep their calendars up to date and indicate their work location (office or remote) on their daily status.

Video should be enabled for all meetings with clients and for team meetings with more than 3 participants. For internal one-on-ones, video is encouraged but not required.

## Workspace Safety

Employees are responsible for maintaining a safe workspace at home. Northbrook Partners is not liable for injuries occurring in the home office but encourages employees to follow ergonomic best practices. Resources are available on the company intranet under "Workplace Safety."

## Policy Review

This policy will be reviewed semi-annually to ensure it continues to meet the needs of the business and our employees. Feedback can be submitted to operations@northbrookpartners.com.
