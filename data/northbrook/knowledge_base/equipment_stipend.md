# Remote Work Equipment Stipend — Process Guide

## Overview

Employees approved for remote or hybrid work are eligible for equipment support per the Remote Work Policy (revised January 2025).

## Stipend Details

| Benefit | Amount | Frequency |
|---------|--------|-----------|
| One-time setup stipend | $1,500 | Upon remote work approval |
| Annual technology refresh | $300 | Each January, starting the calendar year after approval |

## How to Submit

### One-Time Stipend ($1,500)
1. Log into the HR portal at hr.northbrookpartners.com
2. Navigate to "Benefits" → "Remote Work" → "Equipment Stipend"
3. Upload receipts for qualifying purchases (monitor, desk, chair, keyboard, etc.)
4. Submit within 90 days of your remote work start date
5. Reimbursement processed within 2 pay cycles

### Annual Refresh ($300)
- Automatically available each January 1st
- Same submission process as above
- Must be used by December 31st — does not roll over
- First eligible January: the calendar year AFTER your remote work approval date
  - Example: approved October 2024 → first refresh January 2025

## Qualifying Purchases
- Monitors, keyboards, mice, webcams
- Desk, chair, lighting
- Headset or speakers for calls
- Cable management, USB hubs
- NOT eligible: internet service, co-working memberships, software subscriptions

Contact HR Benefits (hr-benefits@northbrookpartners.com) or your HR Business Partner.
