# Northbrook Partners — Paid Time Off Policy

## Purpose

Northbrook Partners recognizes the importance of rest and personal time for maintaining a productive and engaged workforce. This policy outlines the paid time off (PTO) benefits available to all eligible employees. It replaces the previous PTO policy (HR-2021-004) effective January 1, 2023.

## Eligibility

All full-time employees who have completed their 90-day probationary period are eligible for PTO benefits under this policy. Part-time employees working 20 or more hours per week receive prorated benefits based on their scheduled hours relative to a standard 40-hour week.

## Paid Time Off Allowances

### Vacation Days

Each eligible full-time employee receives **17 vacation days** per calendar year. Vacation days accrue on a semi-monthly basis at a rate of approximately 0.708 days per pay period (17 days ÷ 24 pay periods). Employees may not use vacation days before they have accrued. Unused vacation days may be carried over to the following calendar year, up to a maximum of **4 days**. Any accrued vacation beyond the 4-day carryover limit will be forfeited on January 1 of the new year.

Employees who have completed 5 or more years of service receive an additional 3 vacation days (total: 20 days/year). Tenure-based days follow the same accrual and carryover rules.

Vacation requests must be submitted through the HR portal at least two weeks in advance. Requests of five or more consecutive days require manager approval and should be submitted at least 30 days in advance. No more than 3 employees from the same team may be on vacation simultaneously. Requests are approved on a first-come, first-submitted basis via the HR portal. While Northbrook Partners makes every effort to honor vacation requests, approval is subject to business needs and team coverage requirements.

### Sick Days

Each eligible full-time employee receives **7 sick days** per calendar year. Sick days are available in full on January 1 of each year and do not accrue incrementally. Unused sick days do not carry over and are forfeited at year-end. Employees who exhaust their sick day allowance may use vacation days for additional sick time with manager approval.

Employees must notify their direct manager by 9:00 AM on any day they intend to use a sick day. For absences of three or more consecutive days, a physician's note may be required upon return.

### Personal Days

Each eligible full-time employee receives **3 personal days** per calendar year. Personal days are intended for personal business, appointments, or obligations that cannot be scheduled outside of work hours. Personal days are available on January 1 and do not carry over.

Personal day requests should be submitted at least 48 hours in advance when possible.

## Holiday Schedule

In addition to PTO, Northbrook Partners observes the following paid holidays: New Year's Day, Martin Luther King Jr. Day, Presidents' Day, Memorial Day, Independence Day, Labor Day, Thanksgiving Day, the day after Thanksgiving, Christmas Eve, and Christmas Day. Northbrook Partners also observes Founders Day (September 14) as a paid company holiday. When a holiday falls on a weekend, the nearest weekday will be observed.

## Bereavement and Jury Duty

Bereavement leave of up to 3 days is provided for the loss of an immediate family member (spouse, child, parent, sibling). Bereavement leave also includes 1 day for the loss of a pet, introduced in 2022. Jury duty leave is provided as required by law; employees must present the jury summons to HR.

## Policy Violations

Misuse of PTO, including dishonest claims of sick time or unapproved absences, may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination.
