# Building Access & Badge Management

## 250 Innovation Drive — New Building Access

Following the office relocation to 250 Innovation Drive (effective March 3, 2025), all employee badges require reprogramming for the new access control system.

### Badge Reprogramming Process
1. Visit the Security Desk in the main lobby (open 7 AM – 7 PM weekdays)
2. Bring your current badge and a photo ID
3. Badge reprogramming takes approximately 5 minutes
4. You'll receive confirmation of which zones your badge can access

### Access Zones
| Zone | Who Has Access |
|------|---------------|
| Main entrance, lobbies, common areas | All employees |
| Standard office floors | All employees |
| Focus rooms | All employees (badge tap required) |
| Server room / network closets | IT Infrastructure team only |
| Executive floor (4th) | Executive team + EAs |
| Research lab | Research team + approved visitors |

### Temporary Access
If your badge hasn't been reprogrammed yet:
- Request a temporary visitor badge from the Security Desk
- Temporary badges are valid for 5 business days
- Do NOT tailgate other employees — this triggers a security alert

### After Hours Access (7 PM – 7 AM)
After-hours access requires manager approval. Submit a request through the HR portal under "Facilities" → "After Hours Access."

## Lost or Damaged Badges
Report immediately to security@northbrookpartners.com. A replacement badge costs $25 (payroll deduction) and takes 1 business day to issue.
