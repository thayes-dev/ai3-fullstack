# Benefits Enrollment Portal — Access & Troubleshooting

## Portal URL
https://benefits.northbrookpartners.com

## Known Issue: "Session Expired" Error

Some users encounter an immediate "Session Expired" error after logging in. This is typically caused by cached authentication tokens from the previous portal version.

### Fix
1. Clear ALL cookies and site data for `benefits.northbrookpartners.com`
   - Chrome: Settings → Privacy → Cookies → Search "northbrook" → Delete
   - Firefox: Settings → Privacy → Cookies → Search "northbrook" → Remove
   - Edge: Settings → Cookies → Search "northbrook" → Delete
2. Close ALL browser windows (not just the tab)
3. Open a fresh browser window
4. Navigate to the portal and log in again

If clearing cookies doesn't work:
- Try an incognito/private browsing window
- Disable browser extensions temporarily (especially ad blockers and privacy extensions)

## What You Can Change During Open Enrollment
- Medical plan tier (individual, individual+spouse, family)
- Dental plan (basic vs. premium)
- Vision coverage (opt-in/opt-out)
- FSA/HSA contribution amounts
- Life insurance supplemental coverage
